    A Path to Purity - Solutions for Cleaner Water

    01 Reduce Industrial Discharge

    Encourage industries to use eco-friendly processes, treat wastewater before disposal, and recycle water whenever possible.

    02 Promote Sustainable Agriculture

    Advocate for reduced pesticide and fertilizer use, implement buffer zones along water bodies, and encourage organic farming practices.

    03 Waste Management & Recycling

    Educate on proper waste disposal, promote recycling, and reduce single-use plastics to prevent waste from reaching water bodies.

    04 Water Conservation Practices

    Promote water-saving techniques like low-flow fixtures, rainwater harvesting, and efficient irrigation methods to minimize water wastage.

    05 Strengthen Policies & Regulations

    Stricter environmental regulations on pollution control, including penalties for illegal dumping and incentives for green initiatives.
